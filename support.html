Support

Q: How do I create an account on Academate?

A: To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the registration process.

Q: Can I use Academate on multiple devices?

A: Yes, Academate is designed to sync seamlessly across multiple devices. Simply log in to your account on each device.

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: On the login page, click on the "Forgot Password" link, and follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

Q: Is Academate available for Android and iOS?

A: SOON!, Academate will soon be available for both Android and iOS devices. You will be able to download it from the respective app stores.
